About us...

Yottabyte is a craft chocolate and candy retailer located in Smithtown, New York. Our small boutique is located in the historic district of Village of the Branch, in the original Smithtown School house where American Poet Walt Whitman taught.

We have made a commitment to promote socially responsible chocolate and candy makers. We offer complimentary chocolate tastings to our customers to demonstrate the difference between candy chocolate and craft chocolate. Yottabyte shares the stories of social inequality in cacao production, because educating our community about cacao production is the key step to reach our ultimate goal, to sell 100% ethically produced chocolate.

Ingredients

Our Dark - 72%
Cacao beans, pure cane sugar, cocoa butter, sunflower lecithin, soy lecithin, vanilla beans.
*May contain traces of hazelnut, almond, pistachio, walnut.
**Fresh mix with no traces available upon request.

Our White - 31%
Pure cane sugar, cocoa butter, full cream milk, lactose, sunflower lecithin, soy lecithin, vanilla beans.

Our Milk - Custom blend about 32-40%
Cane sugar, cocoa butter, whole milk powder, cocoa mass, sunflower lecithin, vanilla beans.
*May contain traces of hazelnut, almond, pistachio, walnut.
**Fresh mix with no nut traces available upon request.

Our Marshmallow Pops
Chocolate (see above), Marshmallow (Corn Syrup, Sugar, Modified Food Starch [corn], Gelatin, Water, Dextrose, Natural and Artificial Flavor, Tetrasodium Pyrophosphate).

This product is made on production lines that manufacture other products containing milk. it is also made in a facility that uses sesame seeds).
